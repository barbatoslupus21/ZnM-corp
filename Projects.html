VB.NET

Stock Management System

The Stock Management System revolutionizes traditional warehouse operations by replacing the kanban system. In this efficient system, production teams deduct the materials needed for the day, and warehouse personnel simply export the deducted materials. This streamlined process serves as the foundation for issuing materials, optimizing inventory control and minimizing manual handling.

  • Effortless Deduction: Production deducts daily materials without kanban, simplifying the workflow.
  • Automated Records: The system exports deducted materials for accurate issuance, automating inventory control.
  • Minimized Manual Tasks: Streamlining operations reduces manual handling, enhancing efficiency and reducing errors.
